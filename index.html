PROCESS

Getting in touch

/01

The first step in working together is getting in touch. You can contact me through my website or social media channels to discuss your project and goals. We'll schedule a consultation call or meeting to learn more about each other and determine if we're a good fit. During this call, or meeting I will ask question about your business or industry in order to understand the problem and trying to solve it.

Proposal and set up

/02

Once we've established that we're a good fit, I'll provide a detailed proposal outlining the scope of work and timeline. The proposal will include a breakdown of the project phases, the number of revisions included, and the payment schedule. If you're happy with the proposal, we'll set up a project management system, such as Asana or Trello, to keep track of all the tasks and deadlines. This system will allow us to collaborate efficiently and stay organized throughout the project.

Strategy

/03

we'll create a strategy for your website, including design, functionality, and content. We'll discuss your brand identity, color scheme, typography, and other visual elements. We'll also determine the structure and navigation of your website, the calls to action, and the user journey. We'll talk about the content you'll need for your website, such as copy, images, and videos, and how it will be organized.

Implementation

/04

The implementation phase is where the actual web design and development work takes place. I use Webflow to design and develop your website, which allows me to create a customized, responsive, and SEO-friendly website that is easy to update and maintain. I'll start by creating wireframes and mockups of the homepage and inner pages, which will give you an idea of the layout and design. Once you approve the designs, I'll start building the website, adding all the content and functionality we've discussed.

Launch

/05

Before launching your website, we'll thoroughly test it and make any necessary changes. This includes checking for broken links, testing the forms, and ensuring that the website looks and functions correctly on different devices and browsers. We'll also do a final review of the content and make sure it's optimized for search engines. Once we're both happy with the website, we'll set a launch date. On the launch day, your website will go live for the world to see! After launch, I'll provide you with training on how to use and update your website, as well as ongoing support and maintenance options.
